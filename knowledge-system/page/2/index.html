Bulgarian Definite Articles

2023-02-27
In English, a specific cat would be referred by the following: the cat. In Bulgarian, a definite article is attached to the end of a noun: Котка - a cat Котката - the cat The article will vary depending on the gender and ending of word: Ending Article Examples Masculine nouns -ът/-а* мъж ➜ мъжът / мъжа* Feminine nouns -та котка ➜ котката, нощ ➜ нощта Neuter nouns -то кафе ➜ кафето Plural nouns ending in -а or -я -та кафета ➜ кафетата All other plural nouns -те градове ➜ градовете References

Bulgarian Masculine Definite Articles

2023-02-27
There are two different definite articles for masculine words. Words ending in a constant will use the -ът article when they are the subject of the sentence. Words will end with -а when they are the object. Examples: “Мъжът говори” - The man is talking “Аз говоря с мъжа” - I’m talking with the man References

Numeral Plural

2023-02-15
Some masculine words have specials rules, taking a different plural ending. This happens when these words follow a number or expressions such as колко (how many?) or няколко (a few). In these cases, they will end with -a. “един месец” - one month “два месеца” - two months “един ден” - one day “няколко дена*” - a few days This is called the numeral plural or counting form (бройна форма). This only applies to masculine nouns that do not represent people.

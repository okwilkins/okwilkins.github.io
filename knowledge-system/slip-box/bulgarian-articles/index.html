Bulgarian Articles

2023-01-29 [Updated: 2023-02-15] Zettlecasten ID: 20230129211820
Zettle Tags: Language  Bulgarian 

In English there are 2 types of articles:

  • Definite
  • Indefinite There is no definite article in Bulgarian:
  • “жена” and “куче” are used alone to refer to “a woman” and “a dog”:
    • “Ту е жена” - She is a woman
    • “Аз имам куче” - I have a dog

Bulgarian Conjugation

2023-01-29 [Updated: 2023-02-15] Zettlecasten ID: 20230129213905
Zettle Tags: Language  Bulgarian  Verb  Conjugation 

In Bulgarian verbs are conjugated according to the subject (I, you, …) and the tense (past, present, …). This conjugation is applied to the ending of the verb.

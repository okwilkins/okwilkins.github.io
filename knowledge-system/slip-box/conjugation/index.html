Conjugation

2023-01-29 [Updated: 2023-02-15] Zettlecasten ID: 20230129220508
Zettle Tags: Language  Verb 

A verb can be conjugated with reference to the number, person, mood or tense. Examples:

  • I teach English
  • We teach English
  • You teach English
  • He teaches English
  • She teaches English
  • They teach English

Pronoun

2023-01-29 [Updated: 2023-02-15] Zettlecasten ID: 20230129210911-a
Zettle Tags: Language  Noun 

A pronoun is a word that stands in a for noun, often to avoid the need to repeat the same noun over and over. Use this when your reader/listener already knows which nouns you are referring to.

Example:

  • I have a dog. He’s brown and white.

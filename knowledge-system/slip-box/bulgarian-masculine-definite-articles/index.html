    Bulgarian Masculine Definite Articles

    2023-02-27 [Updated: 2023-02-27] Zettlecasten ID: 20230227102234-a
    Zettle Tags: Language  Bulgarian  Definite Article  Bulgarian Articles  Bulgarian Definite Articles 

    There are two different definite articles for masculine words. Words ending in a constant will use the -ът article when they are the subject of the sentence. Words will end with -а when they are the object.

    Examples:

    “Мъжът говори” - The man is talking

    “Аз говоря с мъжа” - I’m talking with the man

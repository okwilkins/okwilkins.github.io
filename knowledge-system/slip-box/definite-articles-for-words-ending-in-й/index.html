Definite Articles For Words Ending In Й

2023-03-28 [Updated: 2023-03-28] Zettlecasten ID: 20230328202959
Zettle Tags: Language  Bulgarian  Bulgarian Definite Articles 

Most words ending in -й will use a -ят/я article. Examples:

  • чай
  • The definite form is чаят when it is the subject of the sentence and чая when it is the object.

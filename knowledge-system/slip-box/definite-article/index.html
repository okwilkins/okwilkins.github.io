Definite Article

2023-01-29 [Updated: 2023-01-29] Zettlecasten ID: 20230129210906
Zettle Tags: Language 

A definite article refers to a specific noun. You could say:

The man’s dog barked at me.

I lent my sister the book when I finished reading it.

I didn’t go to the the party.

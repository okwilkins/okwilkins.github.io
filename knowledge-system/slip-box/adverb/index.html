Adverb

2023-01-29 [Updated: 2023-02-15] Zettlecasten ID: 20230129214603
Zettle Tags: Language  Verb  Adjective 

An adverb is a word that modifies a verb (he sings loudly), an adjective (very tall), another adverb or even a whole sentence (fortunately, I had brought an umbrella).
